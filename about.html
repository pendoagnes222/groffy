About Us

We are a firm with a clear mission and are proud to be a business that does good. Our goal is to transform your shopping experience.
By introducing you to the people that make your food, we wish to help you realize the importance of learning about the thought process,
enthusiasm, and purpose that each of our producers has when producing our goods. We want you to continue thinking about whether
something you buy is beneficial for you, the environment, and the local economy as you make purchases.

This website was created to let you connect with the people who make your food. Additionally, we offer a platform for small, artisanal
producers to sell their goods. Some of our suppliers are really tiny businesses. There are bigger ones. But each and every one of them
on our site has been specifically chosen for you. We consider factors like quality, which includes ingredients and flavor, environmental
effect, which considers manufacturing and packaging practices, and community impact (which includes things like fair pay, and great
conditions for workers).

Our commitment to operating as a business for good extends to small aspects like where we host our servers and how we collaborate
with clients and manufacturers to minimize packaging and recycle materials whenever possible. We think that everyone should have
access to exquisitely grown food. The farmers, chefs, and other providers who help us locate genuinely wonderful food are our heroes.
To put it mildly, the voyage thus far has been thrilling. We have discovered artisan producers all across the area, many of whom began
by operating out of their kitchens. But most importantly, we've discovered a great deal of enthusiasm for natural ingredients that aren't
mass-produced.
